## Role Summary

Senior Machine Learning Engineer building agentic AI systems over large,
heterogeneous log data. The role emphasizes agent components, tools, memory,
prompting, datasets, evaluation, interpretability, reliability, observability,
and production delivery.

## Responsibilities

- Design, implement, and optimize agentic AI components such as tools, memory
  management, and prompts.
- Build and maintain golden datasets with quality and representativeness at
  scale.
- Prototype and evaluate prompting strategies and reasoning chains for model
  reliability and interpretability.
- Collaborate with product, data, and infrastructure teams to deliver AI-powered
  insights.
- Ensure reliability, performance, and observability of deployed agents through
  testing and continuous improvement.
- Deliver incremental, well-tested improvements that improve customer
  experience.

## Required Skills

- 6+ years of hands-on industry experience.
- Computer science, data science, or related degree.
- Agentic AI systems.
- Prompting strategies and reasoning chains.
- Evaluation datasets.
- Reliability, observability, and production testing.
- Product, data, and infrastructure collaboration.

## Preferred Skills

- Log analytics or large-scale heterogeneous data.
- Model interpretability.
- Tool-using AI agents.
