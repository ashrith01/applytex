## Role Summary

Senior Machine Learning Engineer building conversational AI and intelligent
product experiences. The role focuses on LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation,
information retrieval, knowledge extraction, evaluation, privacy-first product
work, and production-quality ML services.

## Responsibilities

- Design, code, train, test, deploy, and iterate on large-scale ML and LLM
  systems across cloud and mobile or edge environments.
- Build intelligent search, document understanding, recommendations, and AI
  assistant experiences.
- Lead LLM workflows such as data curation, prompt engineering, RAG pipelines,
  tool use, agents, fine-tuning, instruction tuning, and LoRA/adapters.
- Build production services for training and serving, including APIs,
  vector/feature stores, and streaming or ETL data pipelines.
- Optimize latency, cost, and quality using quantization, distillation, caching,
  batching, and autoscaling.

## Required Skills

- Machine learning engineering.
- Natural language understanding, information retrieval, knowledge extraction,
  or deep learning.
- LLMs, fine-tuning, prompt engineering, RAG, safety/guardrails, and evaluation.
- Production generative and predictive systems.
- APIs, vector stores, feature stores, streaming, or ETL pipelines.

## Preferred Skills

- LoRA/adapters and instruction tuning.
- Model compression, distillation, and on-device optimization.
- Privacy-first product development.
