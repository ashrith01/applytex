# Dataset Notices

## O*NET

The taxonomy-derived benchmark fixtures use information from the O*NET 30.3
Database by the U.S. Department of Labor, Employment and Training
Administration. O*NET database content is available under CC BY 4.0.
SmartJobApply is not sponsored, endorsed by, or affiliated with USDOL/ETA.

- Database: https://www.onetcenter.org/database.html
- License: https://www.onetcenter.org/license_db.html

## ESCO

ESCO enrichment is optional and runs only from a locally supplied extracted
ESCO export. Raw ESCO files are not committed by this project. Users are
responsible for following the European Commission reuse terms associated with
the downloaded release.

- Download: https://esco.ec.europa.eu/en/use-esco/download

## Current employer postings

Current postings are fetched from public Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby job-board
APIs. Full posting text is retained locally for evaluation for at most 90 days
and is excluded from Git. Only source metadata, hashes, and derived labels are
committed.
